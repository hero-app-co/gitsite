HERO begins with a core group of people with a dream and vision of saving lives by improving the way people get home after a night out. We aim to reshape how the alcohol industry helps their customers get home safely, forever changing the perception of designated driving and responsible decision making.

We are a diverse team that is highly personable and has an innate ability to pivot and adapt to changing circumstances on the fly.

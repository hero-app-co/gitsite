HERO is more than an app, it’s a community of people and businesses supporting the ability to enjoy a night out while having a responsible solution to get home in your pocket. We don’t discriminate when it comes to choices to get home. All options, one spot, for free, including friends and family.

HERO strengthens communities and their responsibility by creating options; our app is the way we connect all of that. We build a local community of users and partners based on common sense and responsible behavior facilitated by simple actions all focused on enhancing good times! Becoming the simplest and most reliable way to get from A to B safely.

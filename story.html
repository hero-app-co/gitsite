HERO began when one of my best friend’s got a DUI while I was at home, and could have easily given him a ride. When I asked him, “Why didn’t you call me?” what he said next really resonated. He stated that he didn’t know I was available and that he would have called 5 or 6 people, potentially waking them or upsetting them before he’d have gotten to my number in his phone. I realized that there was no simple way for me to let those I care about know that I am available and willing to help.

Our team has set out on a mission to provide a solution that breaks down that communication barrier and fundamentally changes the drunk driving culture in America that impacts millions of families each year. We have partnered with leaders in communities, major insurance & alcohol companies, and local businesses to make this a reality. Our goal is to unite communities behind a powerful solution to reduce drunk driving that creates a win win for all involved.

- Alex Crosby
CEO
